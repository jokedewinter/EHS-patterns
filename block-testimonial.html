Play-It-Safe's iAudit application is an excellent tool to document and manage our monthly health and safety inspections.

The convenience of using portable Apple devices to record issues, including a picture, makes identifying, understanding and resolving the issues much more efficient.

Inspection issues are automatically synced over WiFi and consolidated in the OMS allowing us to communicate issues quickly and consistently, hastening the call for action and resolution.

We look forward to using the other features available on the OMS.

Glen Harvey, Chief Compliance & Privacy Officer

Transcontinental Printing, Canada
